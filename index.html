Our Story

Who We Are

Swabalamban Boyz was born in 2024 in Stuttgart, Germany — a group of Nepali friends far from home, united by a shared love of music and a fierce determination to keep their culture alive.

We play Nepali rock and folk music — not as opposites, but as one language. Our songs carry the weight of the hills, the warmth of village fires, and the electricity of a crowd that knows every word.

We started with covers of the songs we grew up loving, and in 2025 released our first original song, Maya K Ho. Five live shows in, we are just getting started.

"Swabalamban" means self-reliance. That's how we built this — independently, honestly, and with everything we have.
