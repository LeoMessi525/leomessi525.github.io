Our Story

Who We Are

Swabalamban Boyz was born in 2024 from a small rehearsal room in Kathmandu, with nothing but a secondhand amplifier, a madal drum, and a shared obsession with making something true.

We play Nepali rock and folk music — not as opposites, but as one language. Our songs carry the weight of the hills, the warmth of village fires, and the electricity of a crowd that knows every word.

Six years later, we've played over 50 live shows, released 3 EPs, and built a community of listeners who believe music can be both deeply rooted and boldly alive.

"Swabalamban" means self-reliance. That's how we built this — independently, honestly, and with everything we have.
